High Touch, High Impact

Assisting America’s top tier veterans in finding purpose as they transition from military careers to civilian life.

Apply

The COMMIT Foundation is fundamentally changing the way veterans approach their transition from the military to the civilian sector.

Misconceptions, skills translation gaps, and culture gaps may hinder many veterans from earning the right job and companies from hiring the right veteran.

Information

COMMIT educates veterans on the value of their knowledge, skills, and abilities. We help you understand how to translate that value to a meaningful civilian career.

Confidence

COMMIT helps veterans regain their individual confidence back. Having worked as a collective unit for a collective mission in the service, we provide the resources for veterans to once again self-identify and understand their unique and personal value.

Imagination

COMMIT enables veterans to adjust their aperture to rethink and redefine what is possible. We encourage veterans to set their goals high, rather than aiming only for what they know or what seems familiar.

We do not use a one-size-fits-all approach. Our flexibility in tailoring authentic and strategic transition solutions meet the exact needs of the exceptional veterans we serve.
